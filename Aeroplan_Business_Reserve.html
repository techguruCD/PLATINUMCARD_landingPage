New American Express® Aeroplan®* Reserve Cardmembers, can earn 5,000 Aeroplan points for each Additional Aeroplan®* Reserve Card with an annual fee added to your account at time of application, up to a maximum of 2 approved Additional fee Cards. That could add up to 10,000 Aeroplan points. Then, earn 7,500 Aeroplan points for each monthly billing period in which you spend $3,000 in net purchases on your Card for the first 6 months of Cardmembership. That could add up to 55,000 Aeroplan points total in the first year!

You can also earn 45,000 Aeroplan points when you spend $9,000 in net purchases between 13 and 16 months of Cardmembership. Thats 100,000 points total!

Don't want to add additional card holders? You can still earn 45,000 by spending $3000 per month over the first six months and then an additional 45,000 by spending another $3000 per month in the first quarter of the second year you have the card for a total of 90,000 points.
